The students who
shape the exhibition.

Behind The Mosaic of Us is a dedicated student team responsible for curating artworks, designing spaces, managing outreach, and shaping the exhibition experience.

How the Team Moves

From first idea to final gallery flow.

01

Gather

Student work, context, and intention are collected into a clear exhibition map.

02

Shape

Layout, sequence, and atmosphere are tuned so every piece has presence.

03

Open

The final experience invites viewers into a living conversation with the work.

The Working Circles

A constellation of students, care, and craft.

Artwork Selection · Exhibition Flow

Curation Circle

The curation circle gathers paintings, sketches, photography, mixed media, digital art, sculpture, and installations into one shared narrative.

Spatial Design · Visual Atmosphere

Design Circle

The design circle shapes the rooms, rhythm, and visual presence of the exhibition so every artwork can breathe within the larger mosaic.

Outreach · Participation · Dialogue

Connection Circle

The connection circle manages outreach, participation, and communication, inviting viewers into dialogue with student voices and ideas.

"United by a shared appreciation for art and storytelling, the team creates a space for dialogue and connection."
